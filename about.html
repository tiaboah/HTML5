Hello! My name is Tiaboah Patience, a dedicated and enthusiastic Level 100 student pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Cyber Security at the prestigious University of Mines and Technology (UMaT) in Tarkwa, Ghana.

I am driven by a deep passion for technology and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the field of digital security. Outside of academics, I enjoy exploring new technologies, meeting new people, and continuously challenging myself to grow. My journey at UMaT has already been incredibly enriching both academically and personally and I look forward to everything that lies ahead.
