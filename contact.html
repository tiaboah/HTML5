About UMaT

The University of Mines and Technology (UMaT) is a public technical university located in Tarkwa, in the Western Region of Ghana. It is one of Ghana's foremost technical universities, known for excellence in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

UMaT is committed to producing graduates who are technically competent, innovative, and ready to contribute to national and global development. The BSc Cyber Security programme is one of its cutting-edge offerings designed to meet the growing demand for digital security professionals.
